HEMPCRETE SPECIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DEFINITION: Composite material of hemp hurd + lime binder

COMPOSITION:
├── Hemp Hurd: 35-40% by weight
│   ├── Source: Industrial hemp (Cannabis sativa L.)
│   ├── Fiber Length: 10-25mm (optimal)
│   ├── Moisture: <15% at mixing
│   └── Organic certification preferred
├── Lime Binder: 20-25% by weight
│   ├── Type: Natural hydraulic lime (NHL 3.5 or NHL 5)
│   ├── OR: Lime + pozzolan mix
│   └── No Portland cement (unless regional necessity)
└── Water: 35-45% by weight (evaporates during cure)

MIXING:
├── Ratio (by volume): 4 parts hurd : 1 part binder : 1.5 parts water
├── Method: Forced-action mixer (pan type preferred)
├── Time: 3-5 minutes until consistent coating
└── Workability Window: 30-60 minutes

APPLICATION:
├── Cast-in-Place: Temporary formwork around structural frame
├── Block: Pre-cast blocks (300×200×100mm typical)
├── Spray: Specialized equipment for large areas
├── Panel: Factory-produced panels (fastest installation)
└── Thickness: 250-450mm depending on climate zone

CURING:
├── Initial Set: 24-48 hours
├── Formwork Removal: 3-7 days
├── Full Carbonation: 6-24 months
├── Optimal Conditions: 10-25°C, 40-80% RH
└── Protection: Keep moist for first 7 days, then breathe

PROPERTIES (CURED - 28 DAYS):
├── Density: 300-500 kg/m³ (vs 2,400 concrete)
├── Compressive Strength: 0.5-1.0 MPa (non-structural, infill only)
├── Thermal Conductivity: 0.06-0.09 W/mK (excellent insulation)
├── Thermal Mass: Significant (8-12 hour thermal lag)
├── Vapor Permeability: 5-8 × 10⁻¹¹ kg/Pa·s·m (highly breathable)
├── Fire Resistance: Euroclass B-s1-d0 (excellent)
├── Acoustic: Excellent absorption (NRC 0.5-0.7)
└── pH: High initially (pest resistant), neutralizes over time

CARBON IMPACT:
├── Hemp Growing: -1.5 to -2.0 tonnes CO2/tonne
├── Lime Production: +0.5 to +0.8 tonnes CO2/tonne
├── Net Result: CARBON NEGATIVE (-0.5 to -1.0 tonnes CO2/m³)
└── Carbonation: Additional CO2 absorption during cure

QUALITY ASSURANCE:
├── Visual: Consistent color, no voids, proper compaction
├── Density: Within 10% of specification
├── Moisture: <15% before finishing
├── Testing: Sample cubes from each batch
└── Documentation: Mix ratios, dates, conditions logged

FINISHING:
├── Interior: Lime plaster (breathable), natural clay paint
├── Exterior: Lime render (breathable), ventilated rainscreen
├── Never: Cement render, vinyl paint (blocks moisture)
└── Detail: All penetrations sealed with compatible materials
