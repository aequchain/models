1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SALADIFRY is a complete, open-source franchise system for self-sustaining food service operations that integrate:

    Healthy customizable food: Salads, wraps, subs, tacos, smoothies
    On-site food production: Vertical farming, rooftop agriculture
    Staff accommodation: Live-work integration
    Circular operations: Zero waste, renewable energy, water recycling
    Equitable economics: Equal compensation, cooperative governance
    Global replicability: Open-source, internationally adaptable

Key Metrics
Dimension	Target
Food Self-Sufficiency	60-80% by ingredient weight
Energy Self-Sufficiency	80-100% (climate dependent)
Water Self-Sufficiency	70-90%
Waste to Landfill	0%
Staff Satisfaction	>90%
Scalability	Pop-up → Campus (5 tiers)

text
